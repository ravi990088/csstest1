Dental Implant

What are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots in your jawbone to support a replacement tooth or bridge. They are made of biocompatible materials, such as titanium, that fuse with your jawbone over time. This process, called osseointegration, creates a strong and stable foundation for your replacement teeth.

Why Choose Dental Implants?

Some of the benefits of dental implants include the following.

Improved appearance

Restored function

Maintained oral health

Durability

Convenience
